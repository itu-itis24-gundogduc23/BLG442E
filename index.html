The Problem

Mining Investments Are Flying Blind

A typical pre-feasibility study costs millions of dollars and takes 1–2 years before anyone knows whether a project is worth pursuing. At $500 per meter of drilling, the math gets uncomfortable fast — and a significant share of those projects never reach a positive NPV.

"Junior and intermediate mining companies, private equity funds, and project finance teams — the people who are actually placing early-stage bets — are largely flying blind. They have real capital at risk and very little in the way of affordable, fast screening tools."

Gold Diggers was built for that gap. We combine machine learning with classical geostatistical methods like Kriging to produce reliable ore grade and spatial distribution estimates from far fewer drill samples than conventional workflows demand.

Workflow

From Raw Data to
Investment Confidence

Our streamlined 4-step process transforms geological data into actionable economic intelligence in a fraction of the time and cost.

01

Upload Geological Data

Import your drilling data, geological surveys, and project parameters through our secure web portal. We support standard mining software formats including Leapfrog Geo and Datamine exports. Our system also cross-references historical data from geologically comparable regions.

02

AI-Driven Analysis

Our machine learning models — trained on historical feasibility datasets — analyze your input alongside strip ratio, recovery rate, and grade estimation data. Classical Kriging is enhanced with AI to perform accurate spatial interpolation with significantly fewer drill points.

03

Probabilistic Modeling

Rather than a single deterministic output, our system generates a range of NPV scenarios with explicit probability distributions. Commodity price sensitivity analysis and jurisdiction risk scoring are automatically integrated into the assessment.

04

Actionable Report

Receive a comprehensive feasibility screening report through our interactive dashboard. Visualize ore distribution maps, NPV probability curves, and risk scenarios — all designed to support faster, more confident capital allocation decisions.
